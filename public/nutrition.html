Key nutrients

  • Folate/folic acid for neural tube development.
  • Iron for increased blood volume and fetal growth.
  • Calcium and vitamin D for bone health.
  • Protein for tissue growth and repair.
  • Omega-3 (DHA) for fetal brain development.

Simple plate guide

Half plate vegetables and fruits, one quarter whole grains, one quarter protein, plus dairy or fortified alternatives.
