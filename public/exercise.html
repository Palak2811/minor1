General advice

  • 150 minutes per week of moderate-intensity activity if cleared by a clinician.
  • Avoid contact/high-fall-risk sports; hydrate and avoid overheating.
  • Stop if dizziness, bleeding, chest pain, or contractions occur.

Trimester tips

  • 1st: Walk, swim, prenatal yoga; manage fatigue and nausea.
  • 2nd: Add light strength training; avoid supine exercises if symptomatic.
  • 3rd: Focus on mobility, breathing, and pelvic floor work.
